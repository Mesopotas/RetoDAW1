Laboral Hazards

Visual Fatigue

Prolonged use of screens can cause vision problems. To avoid these issues, it is recommended to place the screen at a minimum distance of 40 centimeters and with a slight tilt of the lower part, so that our line of sight is not direct. Additionally, it is important to have good lighting to prevent further strain on the eyes.

Poor Posture

Sitting with poor posture can cause muscle fatigue or various pains. It is important to prevent awkward or improper postures that strain the back, arms, or any part of the body, and to avoid staying in the same position for too long.

Falls or Collisions with Objects

Workplaces should be kept clean and organized, with no items obstructing walkways.

Electrical Contact

It is important that electrical equipment is in good condition to prevent any type of problem.

Mental Load

Work-related stress is very common in these types of jobs, so it is important to take measures such as varying tasks, taking several breaks during the workday, and giving workers more freedom in organization to prevent mental overload.
